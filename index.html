Understanding Color Theory

Fundamentals & Applications in UI Design

Primary Colors

Primary colors are the basic colors that cannot be created by mixing other colors.

Red
Yellow
Blue

Secondary Colors

Secondary colors are created by mixing two primary colors.

Green
Purple
Orange

Tertiary Colors

Tertiary colors are formed by mixing a primary color with a secondary color.

Amber
Vermillion
Magenta
Violet
Teal
Chartreuse

Complementary

Complementary colors are opposite each other on the color wheel.

Analogous

Analogous colors sit next to each other and create harmony.

Monochromatic

Monochromatic schemes use different shades of a single color.

Color Psychology

  • Blue – Trust, Calm, Professional
  • Red – Energy, Urgency
  • Green – Nature, Growth
  • Yellow – Happiness, Attention
